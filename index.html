Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications published by AnubixLabs (the "Apps"), including but not limited to the TradeForge suite (Electrical, HVAC, Plumbing, Carpentry), The Artisan's Awl, The Stillmaster, Bladesmith's Codex, Advantage Player Pro, and OptiLure.

No Data Collection

AnubixLabs Apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The Apps do not have a backend server, do not use analytics or tracking SDKs, and do not require account creation or sign-in.

On-Device Features

Some Apps may access device hardware or permissions to provide certain features — for example, the camera or photo library (to attach or watermark an image within a generated document), the flashlight, or on-device measurement tools. Any data accessed or generated through these features is processed and stored entirely on your device. It is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with AnubixLabs or any third party, regardless of which device feature or permission is used.

In-App Purchases

Apps may offer one-time or subscription purchases processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play, and managed via RevenueCat, a third-party purchase infrastructure provider. Purchase transactions are handled directly by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat under their own respective privacy policies. AnubixLabs does not receive or store your payment information.

Children's Privacy

AnubixLabs Apps are not directed at children and do not knowingly collect information from children, as no information is collected from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
